Industry Relative Strength

Weighted multi-timeframe industry momentum and institutional leadership ranking

Industry Relative Strength Logic

Relative score is the performance of stock against the S&P500 ETF
Industries ranked using weighted relative strength across multiple trend windows
Higher RS Score indicates stronger institutional momentum and sustained leadership
Combines short-term acceleration with long-term trend persistence

RS Score Formula

RS_SCORE =   0.30 × RS_7+ 0.25 × RS_21+ 0.20 × RS_50+ 0.15 × RS_100+ 0.10 × RS_200

Color Logic

🟣 Greater than 1.50 → Exceptional leadership
🔵 1.25 to 1.50 → Strong relative strength
🟡 1.00 to 1.25 → Positive trend leadership
🟠 0.85 to 1.00 → Weak / neutral structure
🔴 Below 0.85 → Relative weakness
