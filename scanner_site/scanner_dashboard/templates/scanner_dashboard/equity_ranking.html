Equity Relative Strength vs S&P500
Ranking Logic

RS_SCORE compares stock performance against S&P500 over 7, 21, 50, 100 and 200 day periods.

RS_SCORE = 1 → Equal to market performance
RS_SCORE > 1 → Outperforming market
RS_SCORE < 1 → Underperforming market

RS SCORE:
0.30 × RS_7 + 0.25 × RS_21 + 0.20 × RS_50 + 0.15 × RS_100 + 0.10 × RS_200

Cumulative Return represents long-term trend strength over ~1 year.
