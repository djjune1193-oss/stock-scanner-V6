Structure Logic Overview

Tracks Moving average of price for equity alignment across 10 / 21 / 34 / 50 / 100 / 200 days
MA10 > ALL(strong) : all moving averages bands are in correct order, strong trend
MA10 > ALL (weak) : 10 day moving average is above all other averages but other bands are not in order
MA10: 21-34 : 10 day moving average is between 21 and 34 days, medium trend
MA10: 34-50 : 10 day moving average is between 34 and 50 days, medium-long trend
MA10: 50-100 : 10 day moving average is between 50 and 100 days
MA10: 100-200 : 10 day moving average is between 100 and 200 days
MA10 < 200 : NO trend

Distance% : Percentage different between current close and respective base
