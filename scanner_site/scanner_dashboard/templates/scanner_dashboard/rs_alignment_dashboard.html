RS Alignment Model Overview

This dashboard maps equities into four institutional-style quadrants based on Relative Strength (RS) and trend alignment.

• Strong and Positive Corr → Outperforming market and trade in same direction as S&P 500
• Weak and Positive Corr → Underperforming market and trade in same direction as S&P 500
• Strong and Negative Corr → Outperforming market and trade in opposite direction as S&P 500
• Weak and negative Corr → Underperforming market and trade in opposite direction as S&P 500

Use timeframe filters (7D / 21D / 50D / 200D) to rotate market perspective.
