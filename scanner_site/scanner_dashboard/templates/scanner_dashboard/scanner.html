Strategy Explanation

This scanner searches for bullish reversal setups in stocks that are already trading in a strong uptrend. The goal is to identify pullbacks that may provide favorable entry opportunities. Works best in healthy market.

The conditions used are:

  • Bullish candlestick pattern such as a Bullish Hammer, Bullish Marubozu, or a strong bullish candle.
  • Strong trend alignment: the 21-day moving average is above the 50-day moving average and price is above the 200-day moving average.
  • Moving average pullback: price dips below the 21-day or 34-day moving average but closes back above it.
  • Positive trend momentum: the 50-day moving average slope is positive.
  • Positive stochastic information: the slope of slower stoch (7,10,4) is positive.
  • Recent pullback: at least one lower candle occurred before the signal.

These conditions attempt to identify stocks where buyers step in during short-term pullbacks within a broader uptrend.
